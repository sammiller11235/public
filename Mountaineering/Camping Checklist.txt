Camping Gear Checklist
----------------------
Group Gear
----------
GPS system
walkie-talkies (optional)
beacon (in some cases)

Clothes
--------
Long undies
dryfit underwear
Thick or thin pants depending on conditions
light shorts
2X thick socks
dryfit shirt
sun shirt
rain jacket
half-zip
puffy jacket
light gloves
warm gloves
hat
neck gaiter

Camping
--------
Tent and rain fly
tent stakes
sleeping bag
sleeping pad
camp pillow
camp shoes/approach shoes (optional)
paracord
tarp (optional)

Other
--------
Toilet paper/blue bag
face wipes
toothbrush/paste
playing cards
ibuprofen
melatonin
knee brace
ankle brace
extended battery pack
cooling towel
binoculars
sunscreen
face stick sunscreen
lip balm X2
sunglasses
first aid kit
 - ace wrap
 - alcohol wipes
 - moleskin/leuko tape
 - inhaler
 - SAM splint
extra batteries
compass and map
camp spoon
headlamp
small handheld flashlight
butt plug
hand sanitizer

cooking
------
camp stove
fuel canister (x2 if melting snow for water)
lighter x2
camel back (3liters)
nalgene
dehydrated food
jerky
cliff bars x10
nut mix
summit chocolate
electrolyte tabs
5hr energy shot
dried mangos
protein bars
sandwich

3 day trip ~8000+ calories
2 day trip ~5000+ calories
2-3 liters of water per day, depending on difficulty, water access, temps

pack weight ~30-45 lbs
